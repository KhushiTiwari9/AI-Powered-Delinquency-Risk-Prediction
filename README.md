AI-Powered Delinquency Risk Analysis & Collections Strategy
Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow for predicting customer delinquency risk and designing an AI-powered collections strategy. The analysis includes Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), predictive model planning, business recommendations, and an AI-based collections framework.

The project was completed as part of the Tata Group GenAI Powered Data Analytics Virtual Experience Program (Forage).

Objectives
Analyze customer financial data to identify delinquency risk.
Detect missing values, inconsistencies, and key risk factors.
Design a predictive model for delinquency forecasting.
Recommend data-driven collection strategies.
Propose a responsible AI-powered collections system.

Key Insights
High Credit Utilization increases delinquency risk.
Multiple Missed Payments are strong predictors of default.
High Debt-to-Income Ratio combined with a low Credit Score increases financial risk.
Payment history is one of the strongest indicators of future delinquency.
Recommended Solution

A Logistic Regression model is proposed to classify customers into different delinquency risk categories. High-risk customers can receive proactive interventions such as payment reminders, repayment assistance, and personalized collection strategies while maintaining fairness, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
